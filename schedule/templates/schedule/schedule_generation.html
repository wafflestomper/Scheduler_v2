Generate Schedules

Warning: Generating schedules will delete all existing section assignments. This action cannot be undone.

Before generating schedules, please ensure:

  • You have uploaded all required data (students, teachers, rooms, courses, periods)
  • All students have appropriate grade levels and elective preferences
  • All teachers have their subject specializations and availability set
  • All rooms have capacity and type information
  • All courses have appropriate type, grade level, and teacher eligibility

The scheduling algorithm will:

  • Create sections for all core courses based on grade levels
  • Assign students to core courses for their grade level
  • Create sections for elective courses
  • Assign students to elective courses based on their preferences
  • Ensure no teacher is teaching multiple classes in the same period
  • Ensure no room is double-booked in the same period
  • Ensure no student is assigned to multiple classes in the same period
